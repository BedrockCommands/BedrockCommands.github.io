## A. ❤️ Respect & Etiquette

1. **Maturity & Conduct:** Refrain from jokes or media that could be perceived as offensive, crude, or immature. Keep banter to a minimum, even between friends, to maintain a welcoming atmosphere.

2. **Pings & Mentions:** Do not disturb staff or members with unnecessary pings (including reply pings), especially if requested to stop. Pings are permitted for users with (@) in their name, provided they are not abused.

3. **Respectful Criticism:** Insulting others, their actions, or their creations is prohibited. Constructive feedback is encouraged, provided it is delivered respectfully and appropriately.

4. **Impersonation:** Do not attempt to mimic staff or other members through profile pictures, nicknames, or bio.

5. **Elitism & Mini-moding:** Acts of superiority (elitism) and attempting to enforce rules as a non-staff member (mini-modding) are not allowed.

6. **Harassment & Nuisance:** Obnoxious behavior and intentional disruption are prohibited. If a member asks you to stop a specific behavior, comply immediately.

7. **Negativity & Venting:** Excessive swearing, slandering, and rumor-mongering are prohibited. Personal vents or heavy emotional topics should be kept to private messages.

8. **Provocation:** Do not present personal opinions as objective facts or attempt to enforce them on others. Avoid provocative or 'baiting' statements intended to start arguments.

9. **Privacy & Doxxing:** Protect the privacy of all members. Sharing sensitive private messages to humiliate others or stir drama is prohibited. Doxxing will result in an immediate and permanent ban.

10. **Zero Tolerance:** Abuse, harassment, trolling, bullying, or any form of discriminatory content will not be tolerated under any circumstances.

## B. 🤝 Communication

1. **Language:** Please use English only in public channels to ensure our staff can moderate effectively. Be considerate of those learning the language.

2. **Inclusivity:** Keep conversations welcoming. Avoid excessive inside jokes or discussing private matters that exclude others from participating.

3. **Debates:** While healthy, meaningful debates are encouraged, immature arguments or the discussion of highly controversial/sensitive topics are prohibited.

4. **Conflict Resolution:** If a disagreement escalates, move it to DMs or report it to staff. You are expected to drop the subject immediately in public channels.

5. **Personal Drama:** Do not bring external drama or personal grievances into public chat. For server-related concerns, contact staff. For personal support, please seek appropriate private outlets.

6. **Spam & Disruption:** Avoid out-of-context messages, excessive reactions, or any form of spam that disrupts the flow of conversation.

7. **Channel Integrity:** Ensure your messages are relevant to the specific channel's topic. Use <#1481179983174045826> for all bot commands.

8. **Inappropriate Content:** 'Tainted' jokes or suggestive references (direct or indirect) are not allowed. Maintain a clean environment.

9. **Voice Etiquette:** Do not scream, shout, or intentionally produce loud/disturbing noises in Voice Channels. Playing inappropriate music or audio is strictly prohibited.

10. **Family Friendly Standard:** This is an all-ages community. Any NSFW, sexual, or derogatory media/language will result in immediate action.

## C. 🖼️ Profile & Media Sharing

1. **Profile Standards:** As a family-friendly community, your profile (avatar, status, banner) must be appropriate for all ages. Accounts promoting adult content or violating Discord ToS will be permanently banned.

2. **Account Integrity:** Alt accounts are prohibited without prior staff approval. Malicious activity, including botting or DDoS threats/attacks, will result in a ban and a formal report to Discord Trust & Safety.

3. **Promotional Content:** Unsolicited self-promotion (including via DMs) is not allowed. Community-beneficial content requires staff approval. Exception: Minecraft-related content may be shared in <#1481179704793890816>, and your Minecraft creations can be showcased in <#1481171056747085824> (including your development Discord/socials if you have one).

4. **Media Sensitivity:** Do not post obnoxious, loud, or inappropriate media. Content featuring flashing lights, potential triggers, or spoilers must be spoilered and preceded by a clear warning.

5. **Content Relevancy:** To maintain the quality of our chats, please avoid flooding channels with random memes or media that are unrelated to the community or current discussion.

6. **Crediting & IP:** Always credit original creators when sharing external work. Unauthorized use, art theft, or the breach of content licenses will not be tolerated.

7. **Explicit Content:** Sharing NSFW, gore, or highly suggestive content—whether via text channels or streaming in Voice Channels—is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate removal.

## D. 🚨 Punishments

**First Offense:** Warning

**Second Offense:** 1-Week Timeout

**Third Offense:** 1-Month Timeout

**Fourth Offense:** 3-Month Timeout

**Further violations:** Permanent Ban (No Appeal)

**Category Tracking:** Punishments are based on the category (A, B, or C) violated, rather than individual rules.

**Staff Discretion:** This ladder is a guideline. Moderators may escalate actions based on severity or offer leniency where appropriate.

**Expiration:** Rule violations older than 3 months may be forgiven or removed from your record if shown signs of improvement.

**Spirit of the Rules:** These rules are not exhaustive. Attempting to use loopholes to bypass them is subject to enforcement. Staff reserve the right to act against any behavior damaging to the community environment.

**Discord [Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms/) & [Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines/):** Adhere to them at all times.
